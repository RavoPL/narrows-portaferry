About Us

"The Narrows" is a family run business, with the team made up of Krzysztof, his wife Marzena and their daughters. Along with their helpful staff, they are one big family.

Established in 2007, "The Narrows", which combines a guest house accommodation and a restaurant, is located in the town of Portaferry in Northern Ireland. Its scenic location in the marina as well as breathtaking views out of its apartment windows solidify "The Narrows" in its leading position for bed and breakfast establishments in the region. Krzysztof Piorek, his wife Marzena and their daughters worked tirelessly to provide customers with an unparalleled dining and resting experience found nowhere else on the Emerald Isle.
